Who am I?

I am a regular teen, living in St.Petersburg, Russia. In childhood have tried a lot of hobbies - sambo, pottery, cooking. But all of this is boring and not for me, i realized, and after another film about hackers started learning how to code 😉

How I became a programmer

For several years I didn’t even know which programming language to start with, and went to programming courses in my city. There I spent a whole year studying Visual Basic, and after watching a video about programming on YouTube during that time, I became interested in many other languages and especially web programming.
